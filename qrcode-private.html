QR Code Kit Effective Date: 2025-06-04 Introduction QR Code Kit (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension. Information We Collect We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from users. All data processed by the extension, such as QR code content, user preferences, and history, is stored locally on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties. How We Use Information Local Storage: User preferences and QR code history are saved locally to enhance your experience. Clipboard Access: When you use the copy function, the extension writes data to your clipboard, but this data is not transmitted or stored elsewhere. No Tracking: We do not track your browsing activity or collect analytics data. Third-Party Services This extension does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services. All QR code generation and scanning are performed locally using bundled libraries. Data Sharing We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.
